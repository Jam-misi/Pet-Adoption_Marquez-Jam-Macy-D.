PET ADOPTION FAQ

Can I adopt more than one pet?

FAQ

Some applicants might be approved to adopt multiple pets depending on the situation, particularly if the animal you want to adopt is part of a bonded pair.

Can my adoption application get denied?

FAQ

Yes. Applications can be denied for various reasons, such as not being able to keep the pet indoors, the pet not fitting in well with the household, or other conditions that could negatively affect the health, safety, or well-being of our shelter animals.
